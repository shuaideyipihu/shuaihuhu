## Interpretation Heuristics
- `short-term-strong` + `near-day-high` => strong tape, but can also be extended; do not auto-chase.
- `strong-up` + `up-but-fading` => green day but fading from intraday strength; watch for chase risk.
- `strong-down` + `near-day-low` => weak tape; avoid premature bottom-fishing unless a clear rebound setup forms.
- `down-but-rebounding` => weak day but some recovery from lows; not automatically bullish.
- High-beta AI names should always be judged with catalyst + position + tape, not price alone.
